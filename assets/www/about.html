Developer Conference is a yearly conference for all Linux and JBoss Developers, Admins and Linux users organized by Red Hat Czech Republic, the Fedora and JBoss Community. 2013 DevConf had over 60 talks in 3 different tracks and additional to that 3 more rooms with workshops and hackfests. With around 600 participants DEVCONF.CZ is one of the biggest events about free software in the Czech Republic.

The conference is organized by Red Hat Czech.
